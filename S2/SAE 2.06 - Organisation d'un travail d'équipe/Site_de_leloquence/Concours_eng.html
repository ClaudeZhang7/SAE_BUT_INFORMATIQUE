Report

For several weeks, the students of BUT Informatique et Statistique et Informatique Decisionnelle have been preparing for the eloquence competition organized by the IUT of Villetaneuse. This year 2022, a new edition takes place with the theme: The Metavers. The question the students had to answer was: "The Metaverse, real life is elsewhere?"
For weeks the students of the 2 departments had read extracts from texts concerning the Metavers in class, they had done their own research on their side on this phenomenon. they all had their own opinions on this subject. Each student was for or against the Metavers. We were able to see what a speech contest looked like through a video made by former students from another university.
To be able to prepare well for the eloquence contest, we had spoken in 3 stages:
On Monday, March 28, 2022, each student had to make a teaser of approximately one minute in front of our class. This teaser allowed each candidate to see what was good and bad at the oral and the jury to have an overview of our presentation. It also allowed us to improve thanks to the advice of the jury.
The week of April 04, each student had to speak in front of the jury made up of teachers and volunteer students. This time it was not a teaser that we had to present but our real speech by answering the question: “The Metavers, real life is elsewhere?” Among the promotion of the 2 departments, two students were selected in each class. The best 2 of each group.
On Thursday April 14, 2022, it was the big day of the eloquence contest, our 12 finalists were ready. This time, they had to appear at the IUT amphitheater in front of their classmate and the jury. They were filmed.
At the end of the 12 presentations, the public could if they wanted to do an interview of the 12 candidates for the SAE. The winners had won a fnac voucher worth 100 euros.
