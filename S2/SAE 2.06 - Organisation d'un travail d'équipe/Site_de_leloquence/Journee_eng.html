General Information


Description :

Eloquence Day is a day during which you can see and participate in events related to eloquence.

There will be special Guests, a free meal and a Game !

Guests :

Bertrand Périer :

Big star of the documentary À voix aloud: La Force de la parole in 2016. Recognized as a specialist in public speaking, he teaches this discipline at Sciences Po and at the École des Hautes Etudes Commerciales in Paris. In 2017, he published a book on the subject entitled "La parole est un sport de combat".

Domink Abbas :

Its quality is Coordination and management of teams, production of radio, television, print media, advice on local or international political decision-making, writing of speeches and political analysis are all experiences that I hope to perpetuate in the future. A young talent for eloquence, he was awarded the Paris 1 Grand Prix for eloquence.

Date/Times :

June 17, 2022 from 10 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.

Program :


- 10 a.m. - 3:30 p.m.: Reception of the public

Upon entering the university, go to the Forum so you can see and talk with other people about the exciting subject of eloquence, you can see the organizers of the event in order to ask for clarification on the day or to talk eloquence. Public passages of eloquence contests or Guests can be watched and discussed.
(Guest passages will be taken only if there is approval)
Venue : Forum

- 11:30 a.m. - 12:30 p.m.: Welcome of guests

The moment the guests arrive, later in the day you can go to them to talk about eloquence or various subjects.
Venue : Forum

- 12:30 p.m. - 2:30 p.m.: Performances + Interviews on stage

Guests will be able to perform for you, a passage from one of their performances, and after the guests have finished, there will be Guest Interviews, and a FAQ. The FAQ questions will be chosen by the Guests, among many questions that will be written in the Create the FAQ tab, before the event. Venue : Amphi 5

- 2:30 p.m. - 3:30 p.m.: Meal

Meals will be offered and will be delivered by Just Eat.
Venue : : Forum

-3:30 p.m. - 5:30 p.m.: The Eloquence Game

A non-competitive game where people, or groups of people, registered beforehand on the Registration Game of Eloquence tab, can make a performance on the theme they have chosen in order to raise their opinions and their levels of eloquence, to inspire people who come to see the game.

This is not a competition but a game where the participants will have the right to show their opinions in the way they want, whether in a serious way, with humor, or even telling a story, the participants will have free rein to their imagination.
Venue : Amphi 5
