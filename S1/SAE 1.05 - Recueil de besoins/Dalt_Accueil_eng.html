Dana & Bakary

Story

It is the story of a young woman named: Dana Ribati and of a young man named: Bakary Ouattara. Met in Bakary's bar, during a private party, organized for the release of Dana's YouTube channel, they quickly realized that they were meant to be together after a few years Bakary and Dana finally decides to get married why? It is very simple among all the trips that they have been able to make the only trip they want to make, it is to the other because according to the words of Soren Kiekegaard :
“Marriage is and will remain the most important journey a man can take.”

The day

The wedding ceremony is scheduled for the spring season and the day will begin with a visit to one of New York's wonders, the Empire State Building: a skyscraper measuring 381 meters and having 102 floors. We will then go to the City Clerk to collect the marriage license and finally we will go to the Museum Mile where the wedding reception will be held.
